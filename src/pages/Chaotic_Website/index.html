We are Team Chaotic

ABOUT US

Team Chaotic, founded in 2019, is a competitive Fortnite team focused on cultivating high class talent to deliver superior gaming results. Through our core beliefs of community, culture, and perseverance we have our sights set on one goal: Building a feared Fortnite presence.

Team Chaotic is driven by our innate drive to improve and compete, but above all else, we are committed to our members, to our community, and to our craft. We look to compete in a variety of different Fortnite scenes, from scrims, to tournaments, to casual games.

Recruitment

Are you a talented and motivated Fortnite player? Or can you make great video edits and designs? Then we would love to have you on our team. Below are different positions in Chaotic and how to apply for them.


FORTNITE PLAYER

Elite Fortnite player that looks to improve and compete under the Chaotic name.

FORTNITE PLAYER

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE:

As a Chaotic Fortnite player, you must be talented, willing to improve, and participate in any and all events with Chaotic.

  • Skilled Builder
  • Exceptional Aim
  • Good Stats
  • At least 14
  • Willing to Learn

PROCESS:

  • Send at least five clips through either Instagram, Email, or Xbox

  • Send your GamerTag or Epic Games Name

  • If you are a fit, we will contact you with the next steps


HOW TO APPLY:

If you think you have what it takes to join our team you can contact us below at the bottom of our site.

GFX/VFX CREATOR

First class effects designer willing to edit high quality graphics/videos for Chaotic.

GFX/VFX CREATOR

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE:

  • Great Visuals
  • Appealing Edits
  • Amazing Videos
  • Social Media Presence

PROCESS:

  • Send at least three GFX or one VFX through either Instagram, Email, or Xbox

  • Create one GFX or VFX for Chaotic

  • Apply to be a player as well, we will take you being a GFX/VFX member into consideration

  • If you are a fit, we will contact you with the next steps
